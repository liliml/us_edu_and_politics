Welcome to Our Interactive Map of Educational Attainment in US from 2010-2023

This mapping project is about US political parties and their effect over the years on the education levels of less than 9th grade, no high school diploma, college no degree, associate's degree, bachelor's degree, and master's or higher.

Use the tab switcher at the top to toggle between different education levels.

Hover over any state on the map to see detailed population percentage with certain education level in the info panel at the top-left corner.

The color pallet reflects the political party in power at the time. Additionally, there is a year slider on the left side of the page. It will change the data shown to the corresponding year.
